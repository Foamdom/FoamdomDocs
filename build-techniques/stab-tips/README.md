# Piercing Tips

The concept of a piercing tip covers stab tips for all types weapons designed for thrusting, as well as arrowheads. Because they are generally designed differently than each other, stab tips has the subcategories of spearheads and javelin heads in addition to the generic stab tips you would see on swung weapons.

There are three main parts to any Piercing Tip.

* A Piercing Tip Foundation, which includes the biscuit and foam around/between the biscuit and the stab tip.
* The Piercing Tip Foam itself, which is generally a single piece of foam, but could be multiple layers of foam.
* The Piercing Tip Reinforcement, which both works against the stab tip shearing off the end of the weapon and keeps the tip stable, mitigating roll-over/folding on thrusts that land to keep the compression as linear as possible.

Some foundations rely heavily on a specific biscuit design, but piercing tip designs and reinforcement methods are somewhat interchangeable, and can be built on any viable foundation. The caveats to this would largely be that "long" stab tips, where you have 3/4" or more of foam past the foundation and 2+ inches of foam past the end of the core, will want very stable foundations and will need a higher level of reinforcement to be stable. On the opposite end, something like an arrowhead needs the foundation to follow a few strict rules and doesn't need nearly as much reinforcement to keep the tip stable.

There are a variety of options for each of these parts that serve different levels of being lightweight, being durable, allowing for more foam past the end of the core (softer/friendlier stabs), etc. Some designs for handled weapons call for 0.5" of Stab Tip on a 0.75" Stab Tip Foundation, and can stay stable for a reasonable time with just [house-wrap-tape.md](../../materials/tape/house-wrap-tape.md "mention") acting to keep it stable, while others can handle 1.25" of Stab Tip on a 1.5" foundation via a mix of [strapping-tape.md](../../materials/tape/strapping-tape.md "mention") and [cloth-fabric-tape.md](../../materials/tape/cloth-fabric-tape.md "mention")(specifically low/no stretch Hockey Tapes like Howies).
