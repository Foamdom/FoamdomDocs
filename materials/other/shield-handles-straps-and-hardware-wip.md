# Shield Handles, Straps, and Hardware (WIP)

## Straight Handles

The simplest handles, which you'll find on most Punch Shields. Generally these are made of a straight section of PVC, Wood, Fiberglass, or Carbon Fiber. PVC is often more than enough for this need and is what I would recommend for it 9 times out of 10. See[pvc-pipe.md](pvc-pipe.md "mention")for more information on PVC and sourcing.

## Rigid Bridge Handles

These can be made in a variety of ways. Some are just bulky pull handles with an overmold, some are trowels with closed handles, some are shaped PVC and similar, while others are just 3D printed, and some are just solid wood. Depending on the design of the handle, mounting considerations will be different.

A flexible strap with handle around it or attached to it. Some are loose, some are overmolded and stuck fast to the strap.
